How to Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of your personal data from Restaurant Wizard in any of the following ways:

1. Through the Application

  • Log in to your Restaurant Wizard account
  • Go to Account Settings
  • Click on "Delete Account and Data"
  • Follow the confirmation steps

2. By Email

Send a data deletion request to [Your Contact Email] with:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon your request, we will delete:

  • Your account information
  • Your Facebook connection data
  • Your content and posts history
  • Any other personal data associated with your account

Timeframe

We will process your deletion request within 30 days. We will send you a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
